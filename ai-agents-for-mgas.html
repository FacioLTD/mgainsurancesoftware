What Do We Mean by "AI Agents" for MGAs?

When we say AI agents in an MGA context, we're talking about:

  • Software entities that can take a task,
  • Read and understand relevant documents and data,
  • Follow rules and workflows,
  • And act autonomously (with human oversight) to move work forward.

Examples:

  • Turning unstructured email + PDF submissions into structured, triaged risks
  • Checking a quote against delegated authority limits and triggering referrals
  • Generating risk, premium, and claims bordereaux
  • Monitoring aggregates and alerting when nearing capacity
  • Proposing renewal strategies for specific segments

Think of them as digital operations staff that sit inside or alongside your PAS.

2. Cytora – Agentic AI for Risk Digitization & Intake

What it does

Cytora is a risk digitization platform that transforms messy, unstructured submissions from brokers into structured, decision-ready risks. It uses generative and "agentic" AI to automate intake and route risks appropriately.

Key capabilities:

  • Reads emails, attachments, schedules, and statements of values.
  • Extracts key fields and normalizes them into consistent schemas.
  • Applies rules to prioritise and triage submissions.
  • Integrates with existing PAS / workbench tools.

AI agent angle

  • Cytora's latest release explicitly references agentic AI, orchestrating multiple "digitization agents" to handle complex transactions with explainability.

Where it fits for MGAs

  • Fantastic for MGAs swimming in unstructured submissions and spreadsheets.
  • Sits in front of your PAS; it doesn't replace your core system.

3. hyperexponential / hx Renew – Pricing & Underwriting Agents

What it does

hyperexponential's hx Renew is a pricing decision platform widely adopted by complex commercial (re)insurers and MGAs.

Key capabilities:

  • Sophisticated rating and pricing tools for complex risks.
  • Ability to incorporate proprietary datasets and advanced models.
  • Deployed as an underwriter workbench for pricing decisions.

AI agent angle

  • Acts like an AI-augmented pricing assistant: it helps underwriters explore scenarios, compare rates, and use more data without drowning in spreadsheets.
  • It integrates with PAS and intake tools, but is focused specifically on pricing & decision intelligence, not full policy administration.

Where it fits for MGAs

  • Ideal for MGAs focused on complex commercial lines, portfolio optimization, and advanced rating.
  • Works best as a pricing brain connected to a PAS—again, not as the PAS itself.

4. IntellectAI – Intelligent Underwriting Agents for MGAs

What it does

IntellectAI offers intelligent underwriting solutions for MGAs, emphasizing "expert agents" that help triage opportunities, manage submissions, and improve underwriting efficiency.

Key capabilities:

  • AI-powered opportunity analysis
  • Submission ingestion and risk assessment
  • Underwriting workflow support and triage

AI agent angle

  • Markets "pre-built solutions powered by expert agents" for MGA operations. These agents help underwriters identify attractive opportunities and scale capacity more safely.

Where it fits for MGAs

  • Great for improving front-end underwriting and workflow efficiency.
  • Typically deployed alongside existing PAS / policy admin tools.

5. Outmarket AI – AI Agents for Appetite, Placement & Portfolio

What it does

Outmarket is an AI platform focused on data-driven underwriting & growth for MGAs, with a strong emphasis on appetite, placement, and portfolio management.

Key capabilities:

  • Appetite search and intelligent market placement
  • Risk exposure monitoring
  • Claims trend detection and early warning signals

AI agent angle

  • Uses AI-driven insights and analytics to effectively act as a portfolio and placement agent, helping MGAs attract aligned submissions and manage risk exposure more intelligently.

Where it fits for MGAs

  • Particularly useful for MGAs trying to optimise which business they see and place, not just how they process it internally.

6. Earnix – AI for Pricing & Underwriting Automation

What it does

Earnix is known for real-time pricing and personalization, and has published dedicated guidance on using AI and automation to improve MGA underwriting and resilience.

Key capabilities:

  • AI-driven pricing models
  • Real-time rating and personalization
  • Portfolio analytics and scenario modelling

AI agent angle

  • Think of Earnix as a pricing/underwriting strategy agent: it helps MGAs continuously refine pricing and product strategies using AI.

Where it fits for MGAs

  • Strong for MGAs with high-volume, data-rich portfolios, particularly in personal lines or SME.

7. AllDigital / Athula – AI Underwriting Automation Tool

What it does

AllDigital, in partnership with Athula, launched an AI solution designed to help MGAs and carriers automate underwriting and streamline decision-making.

Key capabilities:

  • AI underwriting workflows
  • Data integration and decision support
  • Focus on scalable underwriting automation

AI agent angle

  • Acts as a rule- and data-driven underwriting agent to reduce manual review and speed up decisions.

Where it fits for MGAs

  • Good example of how AI underwriting tools can slot into existing stacks; still relies on a separate PAS for core policy admin.

8. Selectsys / Similar Submission Automation Platforms

What they do

Vendors like Selectsys focus on AI-powered submission automation for MGAs, wholesalers, and carriers. Their tools read incoming submissions, extract structured data, and sometimes even generate instant quotes.

Key capabilities:

  • Reading unstructured submissions
  • Extracting and validating data
  • Auto-populating quote systems

AI agent angle

  • Operate as submission agents: AI workers that turn messy inbound flow into clean, ready-to-underwrite records.

Where they fit for MGAs

  • Extremely helpful for MGAs drowning in inboxes and PDFs, but again, they sit in front of the PAS.
